## Governability, contestability, legibility

These three terms appear throughout TLC. They are not metaphors.

- **Governability**: The system can be made to follow rules that are declared before execution and auditable after.
- **Contestability**: A user can read, challenge, and repair the system's current assumptions in plain language.
- **Legibility**: The system's behavior and state are visible to users who are not engineers.
