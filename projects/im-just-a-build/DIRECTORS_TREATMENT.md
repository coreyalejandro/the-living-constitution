# Director's Treatment — I'm Just a Build

## Core Concept
The protagonist, **The Build**, is a rigid digital scroll with a C-RSP wax seal. The setting, **Silicon Hill**, is a giant server-rack hill. The video remakes the narrative cadence of a Schoolhouse Rock explainer while teaching the viewer that trustworthy builds emerge through constitutional limits, invariants, verification, and evidence.

## Format
- Duration: 60 seconds
- Resolution: 1080 x 1080
- Frame rate: 12fps
- Medium: Remotion animation

## Act I — The Concept (0s–15s)
- Close-up: The Build sits dejectedly on the lowest step of Silicon Hill.
- Wide shot: The rack rises into the clouds; loose prompts blow away in the wind.
- The Build looks toward a glowing PROD factory.
- A token puzzle snaps into alignment.
- The Build imagines becoming a polished app icon.
- He clutches the Living Constitution.
- The wax seal glows green on the phrase “Constitutionally-Regulated today.”

## Act II — The Mechanism (15s–28s)
- The Build hops rhythmically up the server steps.
- Invariant girders slam into place and stop drift.
- Hallucination Cloud is scanned and rejected.
- Blueprint state matches the build contract exactly.
- Giant CLAUDE.md stone tablet anchors the background.
- Logic Gate locks shut.
- One-Turn Tunnel opens.
- The Build rockets through the tunnel, leaving binary behind.

## Act III — The 10 Pillars (28s–48s)
The music becomes more percussive. The Build passes through ten scanners/gates:
1. Forensic Ingest
2. Normalization
3. Event Extraction
4. Admissibility Gate
5. TLC Adjudication
6. Case File
7. Benchmarks
8. Evals
9. Prevention
10. Research Workbench

Each pillar gets one clear visual metaphor and one readable title moment.

## Act IV — The Resolution (48s–60s)
- The Build emerges as a validated app.
- A hexadecimal hash shield appears.
- The DRAFT sticker peels away and reveals VALIDATED.
- Reliable copies multiply.
- The Build plants a flag atop Silicon Hill.
- Final TLC seal end card.
