# Visual Invariants — I'm Just a Build

## Palette
- Mustard: `#E1AD01`
- Parchment: `#FEF9E7`
- Burnt Orange: `#CC5500`
- Olive: `#4B5320`
- Dark Linework: `#2D2D2D`
- Verification Green Accent: `#2E8B57`

## Non-Negotiable Invariants
- **INVARIANT_VIS_01:** The Build is rigid. It does not squash, stretch, melt, or wobble.
- **INVARIANT_VIS_02:** The C-RSP seal glows green only on verified / constitutionally regulated moments.
- **INVARIANT_VIS_03:** Frame rate is fixed at 12fps.
- **INVARIANT_VIS_04:** Any jitter must be deterministic and frame-seeded.
- **INVARIANT_VIS_05:** No 3D camera moves, no glassmorphism, no neon UI, no blur-heavy modern motion design.
- **INVARIANT_VIS_06:** Text must remain readable at mobile-feed scale.
- **INVARIANT_VIS_07:** Silicon Hill must remain recognizably a server-rack hill, not a generic mountain.
- **INVARIANT_VIS_08:** Each of the 10 pillars must appear with distinct readable labeling.
- **INVARIANT_VIS_09:** The end state must communicate VALIDATED, not merely pretty.

## Motion Rules
- Jitter range: max ±1px translation on selected elements.
- Camera movement: only at major act boundaries and reveals.
- Transition style: analog/cel feel, not slick SaaS motion graphics.
- Tunnel motion: linear and legible, not chaotic.

## Forbidden Elements
- Lens flares
- Particle explosions
- AI-generated hallucination imagery disconnected from the treatment
- Randomized motion
- Unbounded easing
- Unlabeled symbolism
