# Visual Invariants — “I’m Just a Build”

These rules are non-drifting style constraints for the Remotion package. They exist to keep the project visually coherent and to prevent aesthetic drift into a different genre.

## 1. Format Invariants

- Canvas must remain **1080x1080**
- Framerate must remain **12fps**
- Duration must remain **60 seconds**
- Composition must remain legible in square social framing

## 2. Style Invariants

- The piece must remain a **1970s Schoolhouse Rock-inspired cel-animation homage**
- Shapes must remain flat
- Outlines must remain hard and readable
- Motion must feel analog and stepped rather than ultra-smooth and contemporary
- No glossy, glassy, beveled, photoreal, or neon-futurist styling

## 3. Palette Invariants

Use only the core palette family:

- Cream parchment
- Ink black
- Mustard yellow
- Olive green
- Burnt orange
- Muted warm off-white
- Wax red accent

Do not introduce saturated blue/purple tech branding or luminous gradients as dominant color language.

## 4. Character Invariants

The protagonist must remain:

- a parchment/build-contract character
- visibly document-like
- marked with a wax seal
- readable as the build itself, not a generic mascot

Do not redesign the protagonist into a robot, chip, mascot blob, human engineer, or app icon.

## 5. World Invariants

The setting must remain **Silicon Hill**:

- server-rack hillscape
- simplified infrastructure silhouettes
- low-detail educational background language

Do not replace the world with a generic office, cloud abstract, browser UI, or cyberpunk cityscape.

## 6. Motion Invariants

- No `Math.random()` in project source
- All wobble/jitter must be deterministic
- Motion should be modest, legible, and diagrammatic
- No motion-blur spectacle
- No explosive transitions
- No high-energy kinetic typography

## 7. Composition Invariants

- Central subject readability comes first
- Negative space must be preserved
- Each act needs a clearly different layout logic
- Captions must not compete with the protagonist
- Pillar act must feel ordered and countable

## 8. Typography Invariants

- Typography must be bold and highly readable
- Avoid futuristic display fonts
- Avoid decorative excess
- Captions must prioritize clarity over style

## 9. Narrative Invariants

The visual story must still communicate:

- uncertainty
- regulation
- mechanism
- verification
- legitimacy

If a visual change weakens that chain, it violates the project.

## 10. Truth Invariants

Documentation and output reporting must not imply that rendered artifacts exist unless they were actually rendered in the current environment. Source existence and render existence are separate facts and must remain separate in all reporting.
