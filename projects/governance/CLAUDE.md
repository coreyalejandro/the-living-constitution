TLC governance registry overlay under `projects/governance/` (for example `registry.json` and related artifacts). Inherits the root `CLAUDE.md` and `THE_LIVING_CONSTITUTION.md`.

Institutionalization and registry surfaces that tie TLC projects to constitutional controls.

## Rules

All Living Constitution Articles I–V apply. Prefer `MASTER_PROJECT_INVENTORY.json` and root verifiers as the cross-project source of truth unless this folder’s contract says otherwise.
